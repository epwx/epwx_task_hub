**Project Description**

**Social Presence:** [X] Twitter/X campaigns | [TG] Telegram-gated community access | [SHARE] Social reward participation

EPWX is the native utility token of the ePowerX ecosystem, built on Base to support reward distribution, community participation, social engagement campaigns, merchant incentives, and future payment infrastructure. The EPWX Task Hub is the first live utility layer in the ecosystem, allowing users to connect wallets, verify community participation, complete campaigns, and earn EPWX through structured reward flows.

The platform combines wallet-based verification, Telegram-gated access, Twitter/X-linked campaign participation, merchant reward logic, cashback incentives, and transparent token statistics. By using Base, EPWX benefits from low fees, fast transactions, and EVM compatibility, making it suitable for both user-facing rewards and broader ecosystem expansion.

**The ePowerX ecosystem consists of:**

1. ePowerX Token (EPWX): Native utility token used across rewards, campaign incentives, and ecosystem participation.
2. EPWX Task Hub: Live reward platform for daily claims, special claims, social campaigns, cashback, and merchant-linked promotions.
3. Merchant Reward Layer: Promotional reward system designed to support in-store and merchant-based EPWX distribution.
4. Campaign Infrastructure: Tools for advertiser-funded and verifier-backed task campaigns on Base.

**Core Utility of EPWX**

1. Reward Distribution: EPWX is used for daily rewards, campaign rewards, cashback, and special claim incentives.
2. Community Participation: EPWX supports Telegram-gated and campaign-based user engagement.
3. Social Growth: EPWX powers Twitter/X campaign rewards and structured community expansion flows.
4. Merchant Promotion: EPWX is positioned as a customer acquisition and loyalty reward asset for participating merchants.

**Key Benefits**

1. Low-cost transactions: Built on Base for efficient, low-fee reward and payment activity.
2. Fast settlement: Token rewards and ecosystem actions can be processed quickly on-chain.
3. Real utility: EPWX is already integrated into live claim, reward, and campaign systems.
4. Scalable design: The architecture supports future expansion into deeper commerce, campaign, and payment use cases.

**Current Platform Features**

1. Daily EPWX claims with wallet-signature verification.
2. [X] Twitter/X campaign rewards with admin-managed campaign controls.
3. [TG] Telegram verification for gated reward eligibility.
4. Cashback rewards tied to qualifying EPWX purchase activity.
5. Public token data endpoints for total, circulating, and burned supply transparency.

**Why EPWX Matters**

EPWX is designed to move beyond passive token branding by attaching utility to recurring user actions. The project focuses on verified rewards, transparent distribution, community growth, and merchant expansion, with the long-term goal of building a broader Base-native utility and payment ecosystem around ePowerX.
