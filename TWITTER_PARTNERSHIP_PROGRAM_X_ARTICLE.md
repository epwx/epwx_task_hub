# X Article: EPWX Twitter Partnership Program

## Long-Form X Post (Article Style)

Introducing the EPWX Twitter Partnership Program.

If you are growing communities on X and want to turn your audience into on-chain outcomes, this program is built for you.

At EPWX, we now have a dedicated Partner Portal at `/partner` and an Admin Verification Console at `/admin/partners` to make partnerships transparent, secure, and scalable.

### Why We Built This

Most Web3 referral systems are either too loose (easy to game) or too rigid (hard for real creators to use).

Our approach is simple:
- Make onboarding fast for real partners
- Add strong verification for trust
- Track earnings with clear visibility

### Partner Experience (`/partner`)

The partner flow is designed for creators, community leads, and growth operators:

1. Connect wallet
2. Submit partner application
3. Add your X profile and verification details
4. Upload proof (Twitter followers screenshot)
5. Wait for approval status
6. Once approved, access your dashboard, links, and earnings tracking

Inside the dashboard, partners can monitor:
- Total earnings
- Pending and completed rewards
- Referrals and verified claims
- Activity over time

### Admin Experience (`/admin/partners`)

Admins get a focused moderation workflow:

1. Review pending partner applications
2. Validate submitted X proof
3. Approve or reject with reason
4. Keep an auditable trail of who approved and when

This gives the ecosystem a clean balance between open growth and quality control.

### Reward Model

Approved partners earn EPWX when referred users complete verified claim activity. Attribution is tracked by referral links, and reward records are visible in the system.

That means:
- Growth is measurable
- Partner performance is transparent
- Incentives stay aligned with real user participation

### Trust and Anti-Abuse

The program is supported by:
- X account context in user flows
- Telegram and backend verification layers
- Admin approval before partner monetization

So rewards are tied to verifiable activity, not vanity metrics.

### Who Should Apply

- X creators in Web3
- Community managers and KOL teams
- Telegram/X growth operators
- Ecosystem partners driving user acquisition

The future of growth is on-chain, accountable, and community-led.
